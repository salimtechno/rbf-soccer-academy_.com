    At RBF soccer academy, we use football as a tool to educate the young generation to be disciplined, responsible, and productive in society. Football activities make young people choose a different future that is capable of transforming their own lives as well as the lives of the members of their community.

    We believe education and football training, especially for kids, are complementary. We shall continue to strengthen the education pillar for all our academy kids since we want to build a generation of educated and productive sportsmen.We have over 70 children that we train and nurture with their talents. We see all these as opportunities to make real differences in the lives of these kids and the community at large. We want to give hope to those with none, dreams to those without, and a passionate love for football that is shared by players, coaches, academy staff, parents, fans, and everyone in the community.

    RBF Soccer Academy was founded in 2024. The academy is registered with the Uganda Youth Football Association (UYFA), an association mandated to oversee youth football groups and academies in Uganda.

    Programs

    Developing talent is at the core of our training programs. Our training and coaching programs are run by qualified coaching staff certified by the Federation of Uganda Football Association (FUFA)..
